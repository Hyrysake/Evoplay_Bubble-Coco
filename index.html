How to play

1

Aim and shoot bubbles to make matches of three or more of the same color.

2

Clear all bubbles to complete the level.

3

Utilize special bubbles like Bomb, Rainbow, and more to overcome tricky obstacles.

4

Plan your moves strategically to earn the maximum stars.
